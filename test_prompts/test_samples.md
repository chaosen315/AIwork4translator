# 测试样例集

## 样例1：基础科幻文本
Night City was once a shining beacon of technological progress, but now it's a dystopian metropolis where corporations rule and cybernetic enhancements are commonplace.

## 样例2：包含专有名词
The Trauma Team arrived at the Arasaka tower where the Militech agents were holding the hostage. The corporate war between these two megacorps had escalated beyond anyone's expectations.

## 样例3：复杂长句
In the sprawling metropolis of Neo-Kyoto, where the neon-lit streets pulse with the rhythm of a thousand different languages and the air hangs thick with the scent of ramen and ozone, the Oni-killer known only as Raven moved through the shadows like a ghost, her cybernetic eyes scanning the crowds for any sign of the Yakuza hit squad that had been trailing her for three days.

## 样例4：游戏术语
The player character, a street samurai with maxed-out reflexes and a mono-filament katana, encountered a group of gangers in the Combat Zone. The DM asked for an initiative roll, and the player's d10 came up 7, giving him the edge in the upcoming firefight.

## 样例5：混合文化元素
The korova milk bar was filled with droogs wearing the latest in moloko-plus fashion, while outside, the streets of A Clockwork Orange's dystopian London were patrolled by millicops armed with truncheons and bad attitudes.
